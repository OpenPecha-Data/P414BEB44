samamātmānamālokya yataḥ svādhikyavṛddhaye|kalahenāpi saṃsādhyaṃ lābhasatkāramātmanaḥ

api sarvatra me loke bhaveyuḥ prakaṭā guṇāḥ|api nāma guṇā ye'sya na śroṣyantyapi kecana

chādyerannapi me doṣāḥ syānme pūjāsya no bhavet|sulabdhā adya me lābhāḥ pūjito'hamayaṃ na tu

paśyāmo muditāstāvaccirādenaṃ khalīkṛtam|hāsyaṃ janasya sarvasya nindyamānamitastataḥ

asyāpi hi varākasya spardhā kila mayā saha|kimasya śrutametāvat prajñā rūpaṃ kulaṃ dhanam

evamātmaguṇān śrutvā kīrtyamānānitastataḥ|saṃjātapulako hṛṣṭaḥ paribhokṣye sukhotsavam

yadyapyasya bhavellābho grāhyo'smābhirasau balāt|datvāsmai yāpanāmātramasmatkarma karoti cet

sukhācca cyāvanīyo'yaṃ yojyo'smadvyathayā sadā|anena śataśaḥ sarve saṃsāravyathitā vayam

aprameyā gatāḥ kalpāḥ svārthaṃ jijñāsatastava|śrameṇa mahatānena duḥkhameva tvayārjitam

madvijñaptyā tathātrāpi pravartasvāvicārataḥ|drakṣyasyetadguṇān paścādbhūtaṃ hi vacanaṃ muneḥ

abhaviṣyadidaṃ karma kṛtaṃ pūrvaṃ yadi tvayā|bauddhaṃ saṃpatsukhaṃ muktvā nābhaviṣyadiyaṃ daśā

tasmādyathānyadīyeṣu śukraśoṇitabinduṣu|cakartha tvamahaṃkāraṃ tathānyeṣvapi bhāvaya

anyadīyaścaro bhūtvā kāye'smin yadyadīkṣase|tattadevāpahṛtyarthaṃ parebhyo hitamācara

ayaṃ susthaḥ paro duḥstho nīcairanyo'yamuccakaiḥ|paraḥ karotyayaṃ neti kuruṣverṣyāṃ tvamātmani

sukhācca cyāvayātmānaṃ paraduḥkhe niyojaya|kadāyaṃ kiṃ karotīti chala(phala)masya nirūpaya

anyenāpi kṛtaṃ doṣaṃ pātayāsyaiva mastake|alpamapyasya doṣaṃ ca prakāśaya mahāmuneḥ

anyādhikayaśovādairyaśo'sya malinīkuru|nikṛṣṭadāsavaccainaṃ sattvakāryeṣu vāhaya

nāgantukaguṇāṃśena stutyo doṣamayo hyayam|yathā kaścinna jānīyādguṇamasya tathā kuru

saṃkṣepādyadyadātmārthe pareṣvapakṛtaṃ tvayā|tattadātmani sattvārthe vyasanaṃ vinipātaya

naivotsāho'sya dātavyo yenāyaṃ mukharo bhavet|sthāpyo navavadhūvṛttau hrīto bhīto'tha saṃvṛtaḥ

evaṃ kuruṣva tiṣṭhaivaṃ na kartavyamidaṃ tvayā|evameva vaśaḥ kāryo nigrāhyastadatikrame

athaivamucyamāne'pi citta nedaṃ kariṣyasi|tvāmeva nigrahīṣyāmi sarvadoṣāstvadāśritāḥ

kva yāsyasi mayā dṛṣṭaḥ sarvadarpānnihanmi te|anyo'sau pūrvakaḥ kālastvayā yatrāsmi nāśitaḥ

adyāpyasti mama svārtha ityāśāṃ tyaja sāṃpratam|tvaṃ vikrīto mayānyeṣu bahukhedamacintayan

tvāṃ sattveṣu na dāsyāmi yadi nāma pramodataḥ|tvaṃ māṃ narakapāleṣu pradāsyasi na saṃśayaḥ

evaṃ cānekadhā datvā tvayāhaṃ vyathitaściram|nihanmi svārthaceṭaṃ tvāṃ tāni vairāṇyanusmaran

na kartavyātmani prītiryadyātmaprītirasti te|yadyātmā rakṣitavyo'yaṃ rakṣitavyo na yujyate

yathā yathāsya kāyasya kriyate paripālanam|sukumārataro bhūtvā patatyeva tathā tathā

asyaivaṃ patitasyāpi sarvāpīyaṃ vasuṃdharā|nālaṃ pūrayituṃ vāñchāṃ tatko'syecchāṃ kariṣyati

aśakyamicchataḥ kleśa āśābhaṅgaśca jāyate|nirāśo yastu sarvatra tasya saṃpadajīrṇikā

tasmānna prasaro deyaḥ kāyasyecchābhivṛddhaye|bhadrakaṃ nāma tadvastu yadiṣṭatvānna gṛhyate

bhasmaniṣṭhāvasāneyaṃ niśceṣṭānyena cālyate|aśucipratimā ghorā kasmādatra mamāgrahaḥ

kiṃ mamānena yantreṇa jīvinā vā mṛtena vā|loṣṭādeḥ ko viśeṣo'sya hāhaṃkāraṃ na naśyasi

śarīrapakṣapātena vṛthā duḥkhamupārjyate|kimasya kāṣṭhatulyasya dveṣeṇānunayena vā

mayā vā pālitasyaivaṃ gṛdhrādyairbhakṣitasya vā|na ca sneho na ca dveṣastatra snehaṃ karomi
kim

roṣo yasya khalīkārāttoṣo yasya ca pūjayā|sa eva cenna jānāti śramaḥ kasya kṛtena me

imaṃ ye kāyamicchanti te'pi me suhṛdaḥ kila|sarve svakāyamicchanti te'pi kasmānna me priyāḥ

tasmānmayānapekṣeṇa kāyastyakto jagaddhite|ato'yaṃ bahudoṣo'pi dhāryate karmabhāṇḍavat

tenālaṃ lokacaritaiḥ paṇḍitānanuyāmyaham|apramādakathāṃ smṛtvā styānamiddhaṃ nivārayan

tasmādāvaraṇaṃ hantuṃ samādhānaṃ karomyaham|vimārgāccittamākṛṣya svālambananirantaram

bodhicaryāvatāre dhyānapāramitā nāma aṣṭamaḥ paricchedaḥ|
