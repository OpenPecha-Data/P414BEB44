|evaṃ kṣamo bhajedvīryaṃ vīrye bodhiryataḥ sthitā|na hi vīryaṃ vinā puṇyaṃ yathā vāyuṃ vināgatiḥ

kiṃ vīryaṃ kuśalotsāhastadvipakṣaḥ ka ucyate|ālasyaṃ kutsitāsaktirviṣādātmāvamanyanā

avyāpārasukhāsvādanidrāpāśrayatṛṣṇayā|saṃsāraduḥkhānudvegādālasyamupajāyate

kleśavāgurikāghrātaḥ praviṣṭo janmavāgurām|kimadyāpi na jānāsi mṛtyorvadanamāgataḥ

svayūthyānmāryamāṇāṃstvaṃ krameṇaiva na paśyasi|tathāpi nidrāṃ yāsyeva caṇḍālamahiṣo yathā

yamenodvīkṣyamāṇasya baddhamārgasya sarvataḥ|kathaṃ te rocate bhoktuṃ kathaṃ nidrā kathaṃ ratiḥ

yāvatsaṃbhṛtasaṃbhāraṃ maraṇaṃ śīghrameṣyati|saṃtyajyāpi tadālasyamakāle kiṃ kariṣyasi

idaṃ na prāptamārabdhamidamardhakṛtaṃ sthitam|akasmānmṛtyurāyāto hā hato'smīti cintayan

śokavegasamucchūnasāśruraktekṣaṇānanān|bandhūnnirāśān saṃpaśyan yamadūtamukhāni ca

svapāpasmṛtisaṃtaptaḥ śṛṇvannādāṃśca nārakān|trāsoccāraviliptāṅgo vihvalaḥ kiṃ kariṣyasi

jīvamatsya ivāsmīti yuktaṃ bhayamihaiva te|kiṃ punaḥ kṛtapāpasya tīvrānnarakaduḥkhataḥ

spṛṣṭa uṣṇodakenāpi sukumāra pratapyase|kṛtvā ca nārakaṃ karma kimevaṃ svasthamāsyate

nirudyama phalākāṅkṣin sukumāra bahuvyatha|mṛtyugrasto'marākāra hā duḥkhita vihanyase

mānuṣyaṃ nāvamāsādya tara duḥkhamahānadīm|mūḍha kālo na nidrāyā iyaṃ naurdurlabhā punaḥ

muktvā dharmaratiṃ śreṣṭhāmanantaratisaṃtatim|ratirauddhatyahāsyādau duḥkhahetau kathaṃ tava

aviṣādabalavyūhatātparyātmavidheyatā|parātmasamatā caiva parātmaparivartanam

naivāvasādaḥ kartavyaḥ kuto me bodhirityataḥ|yasmāttathāgataḥ satyaṃ satyavādīdamuktavān

te'pyāsan daṃśamaśakā makṣikāḥ kṛmayastathā|yairutsāhavaśāt prāptā durāpā bodhiruttamā

kimutāhaṃ naro jātyā śakto jñātuṃ hitāhitam|sarvajñanītyanutsargādbodhiṃ kiṃ nāpnuyāmaham

athāpi hastapādādi dātavyamiti me bhayam|gurulāghavamūḍhatvaṃ tanme syādavicārataḥ

chettavyaścāsmi bhettavyo dāhyaḥ pāṭyo'pyanekaśaḥ|kalpakoṭīrasaṃkhyeyā na ca bodhirbhaviṣyati

idaṃ tu me parimitaṃ duḥkhaṃ saṃbodhisādhanam|naṣṭaśalyavyathāpohe tadutpādanaduḥkhavat

sarve'pi vaidyāḥ kurvanti kriyāduḥkhairarogatām|tasmādbahūni duḥkhāni hantuṃ soḍhavyamalpakam

kriyāmimāmapyucitāṃ varavaidyo na dattavān|madhureṇopacāreṇa cikitsati mahāturān

ādau śākādidāne'pi niyojayati nāyakaḥ|tatkaroti kramātpaścādyatsvamāṃsānyapi tyajet

yadā śākeṣviva prajñā svamāṃse'pyupajāyate|māṃsāsthi tyajatastasya tadā kiṃ nāma duṣkaram

na duḥkhī tyaktapāpatvātpaṇḍitatvānna durmanāḥ|mithyākalpanayā citte pāpātkāye yato vyathā

puṇyena kāyaḥ sukhitaḥ pāṇḍityena manaḥ sukhi|tiṣṭhan parārthaṃ saṃsāre kṛpāluḥ kena khidyate

kṣapayan pūrvapāpāni pratīcchan puṇyasāgarān|bodhicittabalādeva śrāvakebhyo'pi śīghragaḥ

evaṃ sukhātsukhaṃ gacchan ko viṣīdetsacetanaḥ|bodhicittarathaṃ prāpya sarvakhedaśramāpaham

chandasthāmaratimuktibalaṃ sattvārthasiddhaye|chandaṃ duḥkhabhayātkuryādanuśaṃsāṃśca bhāvayan

evaṃ vipakṣamunmūlya yatetotsāhavṛddhaye|chandamānaratityāgatātparyavaśitābalaiḥ

aprameyā mayā doṣā hantavyāḥ svaparātmanoḥ|ekaikasyāpi doṣasya yatra kalpārṇavaiḥ kṣayaḥ

tatra doṣakṣayārambhe leśo'pi mama nekṣyate|aprameyavyathābhājye noraḥ sphuṭati me katham

guṇā mayārjanīyāśca bahavaḥ svaparātmanoḥ|tatraikaikaguṇābhyāso bhavetkalpārṇavairna vā

guṇaleśe'pi nābhyāso mama jātaḥ kadācana|vṛthā nītaṃ mayā janma kathaṃcillabdhamadbhutam

na prāptaṃ bhagavatpūjāmahotsavasukhaṃ mayā|na kṛtā śāsane kārā daridrāśā na pūritā

bhītebhyo nābhayaṃ dattamārtā na sukhinaḥ kṛtāḥ|duḥkhāya kevalaṃ māturgato'smi garbhaśalyatām

dharmacchandaviyogena paurvikeṇa mamādhunā|vipattirīdṛśī jātā ko dharme chandamutsṛjet

kuśalānāṃ ca sarveṣāṃ chandaṃ mūlaṃ munirjagau|tasyāpi mūlaṃ satataṃ vipākaphalabhāvanā

duḥkhāni daurmanasyāni bhayāni vividhāni ca|abhilāṣavighātāśca jāyante pāpakāriṇām

manorathaḥ śubhakṛtāṃ yatra yatraiva gacchati|tatra tatraiva tatpuṇyaiḥ phalārgheṇābhipūjyate

pāpakārisukhecchā tu yatra yatraiva gacchati|tatra tatraiva tatpāpairduḥkhaśastrairvihanyate

vipulasugandhiśītalasaroruhagarbhagatāmadhurajinasvarāśanakṛtopacitadyutayaḥ|munikarabodhitāmbujavinirgatasadvapuṣaḥsugatasutā
bhavanti sugatasya puraḥ kuśalaiḥ

yamapuruṣāpanītasakalacchavirārtaravohutavahatāpavidrutakatāmraniṣiktatanuḥ|jvaladasiśaktighātaśataśātitamāṃsadalaḥpatati
sutaptalohadharaṇīṣvaśubhairbahuśaḥ

tasmātkāryaḥ śubhacchando bhāvayitvaivamādarāt|vajradhvajasthavidhinā mānaṃ tvārabhya bhāvayet

pūrvaṃ nirūpya sāmagrīmārabhennārabheta vā|anārambho varaṃ nāma na tvārabhya nivartanam

janmāntare'pi so'bhyāsaḥ pāpādduḥkhaṃ ca vardhate|anyacca kāryakālaṃ ca hīnaṃ tacca na sādhitam

triṣu māno vidhātavyaḥ karmopakleśaśaktiṣu|mayaivaikena kartavyamityeṣā karmamānitā

kleśasvatantro loko'yaṃ na kṣamaḥ svārthasādhane|tasmānmayaiṣāṃ kartavyaṃ nāśakto'haṃ yathā
janaḥ

nīcaṃ karma karotyanyaḥ kathaṃ mayyapi tiṣṭhati|mānāccenna karomyetanmāno naśyatu me varam

mṛtaṃ duṇḍubhamāsādya kāko'pi garuḍāyate|āpadābādhate'lpāpi mano me yadi durbalam

viṣādakṛtaniśceṣṭe āpadaḥ sukarā nanu|vyutthitaśceṣṭamānastu mahatāmapi durjayaḥ

tasmāddṛḍhena cittena karomyāpadamāpadaḥ|trailokyavijigīṣutvaṃ hāsyamāpajjitasya me

mayā hi sarvaṃ jetavyamahaṃ jeyo na kenacit|mayaiṣa māno voḍhavyo jinasiṃhasuto hyaham

ye sattvā mānavijitā varakāste na māninaḥ|mānī śatruvaśaṃ naiti mānaśatruvaśāśca te

mānena durgatiṃ nītā mānuṣye'pi hatotsavāḥ|parapiṇḍāśino dāsā mūrkhā durdarśanāḥ kṛśā

sarvataḥ paribhūtāśca mānastabdhāstapasvinaḥ|te'pi cenmānināṃ madhye dīnāstu vada kīdṛśāḥ

te mānino vijayinaśca ta eva śūrāye mānaśatruvijayāya vahanti mānam|ye taṃ sphurantamapi mānaripuṃ
nihatyakāmaṃ jane jayaphalaṃ pratipādayanti

saṃkleśapakṣamadhyastho bhaveddṛptaḥ sahasraśaḥ|dūryodhanaḥ kleśagaṇaiḥ siṃho mṛgagaṇairiva

mahatsvapi hi kṛcchreṣu na rasaṃ cakṣurīkṣate|evaṃ kṛcchramapi prāpya na kleśavaśago bhavet

yadevāpadyate karma tatkarmavyasanī bhavet|tatkarmaśauṇḍo'tṛptātmā krīḍāphalasukhepsuvat

sukhārthaṃ kriyate karma tathāpi syānna vā sukham|karmaiva tu sukhaṃ yasya niṣkarmā sa sukhī
katham

kāmairna tṛptiḥ saṃsāre kṣuradhārāmadhūpamaiḥ|puṇyāmṛtaiḥ kathaṃ tṛptirvipākamadhuraiḥ śivaiḥ

tasmātkarmāvasāne'pi nimajjettatra karmaṇi|yathā madhyāhnasaṃtapta ādau prāptasarāḥ karī

balanāśānubandhe tu punaḥ kartuṃ parityajet|susamāptaṃ ca tanmuñceduttarottaratṛṣṇayā

kleśaprahārān saṃrakṣet kleśāṃśca prahareddṛḍham|khaḍgayuddhamivāpannaḥ śikṣitenāriṇā saha

tatra khaḍgaṃ yathā bhraṣṭaṃ gṛhṇīyātsabhayastvaran|smṛtikhaḍgaṃ tathā bhraṣṭaṃ gṛhṇīyānnarakān
smaran

viṣaṃ rūdhiramāsādya prasarpati yathā tanau|tathaiva cchidramāsādya doṣaścitte prasarpati

tailapātradharo yadvadasihastairadhiṣṭhitaḥ|skhalite maraṇatrāsāttatparaḥ syāttathā vratī

tasmādutsaṅgage sarpe yathottiṣṭhati satvaram|nidrālasyāgame tadvat pratikurvīta satvaram

ekaikasmiṃśchale suṣṭhu paritapya vicintayet|kathaṃ karomi yenedaṃ punarme na bhavediti

saṃsargaṃ karma vā prāptamicchedetena hetunā|kathaṃ nāmāsvavasthāsu smṛtyubhyāso bhavediti

laghuṃ kuryāttathātmānamapramādakathāṃ smaran|karmāgamādyathā pūrvaṃ sajjaḥ sarvatra vartate

yathaiva tūlakaṃ vāyorgamanāgamane vaśam|tathotsāhavaśaṃ yāyādṛddhiścaivaṃ samṛdhyati

iti prajñākaramativiracitāyāṃ bodhicaryāvatārapañjikāyāṃvīryapāramitā nāma saptamaḥ paricchedaḥ||
